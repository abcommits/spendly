Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the Service, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly experience. Specifically, we use it to display your expense history, generate spending summaries, and personalize your dashboard. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers. Any third-party libraries used within the app operate locally and do not transmit your data externally.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also export your expense data or close your account entirely. To exercise any of these rights, use the account settings within the app or contact us directly.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly.
